Please read the instructions carefully. It is important that you understand all the instructions because the amount of money you earn will depend on your understanding of the instructions and your performance in the study. Today’s study will take approximately 15 minutes of your time.

For this study, we will use an experimental currency called “Lira”. The Lira you earn will be converted into dollars. Every 50 Lira you earn will be converted into $1.

Note: At no time will any deception be used in this study, all instructions will be faithfully executed in accordance with the review of the Institutional Review Board.

Task Instructions

In today’s study, you will work on a task known as a "decoding task". In the decoding task, you will decode numbers into letters using the decoding keys in the grid provided on the computer screen, as depicted below. In each period, the key will be different.

Below you see a random grid with a decoding key assigning the 26 alphabetic characters to two-digit numbers. Your task consists of “decoding” numbers by filling the corresponding alphabetic character from the decoding key into the free space next to the number that has to be decoded.

Example: 61 would be decoded to D using the decoding key provided below.

Answers are not case sensitive. After you type in the letter you decode, please make sure you click the SUBMIT button to submit the answer. If you do not click the SUBMIT button to submit the answer, the answer will not be recorded and will not count toward your performance.

You will work on the decoding task for five periods and every period will last two minutes.

Compensation

You will earn 2 Lira for every correct grid you submit in every period. Your total earnings from the study will equal to the sum of your earnings in the five periods.

Practice Period

After you have completed reading the instructions, you will have 30 seconds to practice the decoding task.

Note: Your performance in the practice round will NOT be paid, but the practice round provides you a good opportunity to become familiar with the task.
